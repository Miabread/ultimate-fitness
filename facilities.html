Welcome to the Facilities Tour of Ultimate Fitness


The Ultimate Fitness state-of-the-art facility consists of
more than 20,000 square feet of health and fitness equipment
space. The facility features one free weight gym area,two
aerobics rooms, a cardio room, a sauna room and Jacuzzi
pool. We also have seperate men's and women's locker rooms
that contain lockers, changing rooms, and shower areas.

  • Free Weights
  • Group Cycling(Spinning)

  • Men's and Women's Locker Rooms

  • Stationary Cycling
